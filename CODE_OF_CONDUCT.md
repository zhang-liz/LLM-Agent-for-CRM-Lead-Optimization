# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility and learning from mistakes
- Prioritizing what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Sexualized language or imagery; unwelcome attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior and may take fair corrective action in response to
inappropriate conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an
individual is officially representing the project.

## Enforcement

To report abusive or unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers at:
`maintainers@leadloop.dev`.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
